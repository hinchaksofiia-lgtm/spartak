# Spartak Project

## Project Overview

The Spartak project explores the rich history and intricate details of cake, focusing on its evolution, variations, and significance in different cultures. The project aims to provide an in-depth understanding of not just the recipes but also the traditions associated with cake-making and consumption.

## Features

- **Comprehensive History**: A detailed timeline of the evolution of cake through ages.
- **Regional Variations**: Exploration of different types of cakes from around the world, including recipes and cultural significance.
- **Interactive Recipes**: Step-by-step guidelines to create various cakes, highlighting techniques and tips.
- **Multimedia Integration**: Inclusion of images and videos to enhance the learning experience.
